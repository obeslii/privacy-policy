Privacy Policy

App Name: Nutrai
Last Updated: May 14, 2025

1. Data Collection

This app uses the device's camera to capture photos. The captured images are sent to the OpenAI API for AI-based processing and content generation.
No personal information (such as name, email address, location, etc.) is collected or stored by the app.

2. Data Usage

  • Photos are only used temporarily for processing through the OpenAI API.
  • The app does not save or store any images either locally or on external servers.
  • Once processed, the images are discarded and not retained.

3. Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI API: Photos are sent to OpenAI's system for image analysis. OpenAI's own privacy policy applies.
  • Google AdMob: The app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may use cookies or device identifiers to serve personalized ads.
    Learn more about AdMob: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Data Security

No personal or sensitive data is permanently stored. All communications with external services are transmitted securely using HTTPS.

5. User Rights

Users may uninstall the app at any time. No user data is retained after deletion.
